--- title: Northswiss Jekyll Boilerplate ---

This boilerplate was made to stand on the shoulders of two giants. The first being the standard Jekyll framework, and the second being the HTML5 Boilerplate template. Also, recently added is Bourbon and Neat, so that you can now build sites easily using Neats intuitive grid structure.

On top of this, I have also baked in some standard goodies that I use to help streamline time to production. Please feel free to fork this project and suggest future improvements.
